Commercial Strategy: Channel & Product Mix

B2B First Approach with Channel Partner Ecosystem

Product Portfolio Strategy

Primary (Hardware): Servers, Storage, Networking (HPE, Dell EMC, Cisco). Target: new manufacturing plants needing IT backbones. Aligns with manufacturing boom/FDI influx.
Secondary (Software): Cloud & Security (Microsoft, VMware). Cybersecurity Law 2026 drives demand for compliant security software.
Tertiary (Consumer): High-end gaming laptops/accessories. Fragmented availability. Premium margins without direct competition (Mobile World, FPT Shop).

Rationale: B2C consolidated by giants. Price competition = race to bottom. Leverage Enterprise Solutions with value-added services.

Channel Partner Ecosystem

Target Partners: Build SI network (not just retailers). Target mid-sized SIs underserved by FPT Synnex. Service SME/mid-market manufacturing.

Smart Partner Program:

  • Credit: 30-45 day terms (backed by Singapore hub trade finance)
  • Training: Free technical certification for SI engineers
  • Rebates: Backend rebates on quarterly growth targets
  • Marketing: Co-marketing & lead generation
  • Support: Pre-sales consultation & post-sales service
