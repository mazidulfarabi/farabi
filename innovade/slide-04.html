Evaluating Market Entry Barriers from Bangladesh Lens

CAGE Framework: Distance Analysis

Cultural Distance (Medium-High)

Language: Significant barrier vs Philippines/Malaysia. Business docs require Vietnamese.

Religion: BD Muslim-majority vs Vietnam secular/Buddhist. Less critical for B2B.

Implication: Invest in local management & translators. Manageable via staffing.

Administrative Distance (Low-Medium)

Political: Both centralized. Vietnam's one-party system offers policy stability.

Trade: FTA pursuit, $1B+ trade volume. DTI Law 2026 creates transparent framework.

Regulatory: 100% foreign ownership allowed (unlike Malaysia's 30% requirement).

Geographic Distance (Medium)

Logistics: Closer to East Asia manufacturing (China, Taiwan, Korea). Faster/cheaper imports vs Dhaka/Manila.

Connectivity: Expanding air links improve executive mobility.

Regional Hubs: Singapore hub serves as strategic intermediary, optimizing costs.

Economic Distance (Optimal)

Structural: Vietnam's industrializing, export-led economy mirrors BD trajectory. Experience serving garment factories/govt projects transferable to electronics factories/smart cities.

Market Maturity: Similar digital transformation stages create natural demand for value-added services.
