Phase Milestone Key Actions & Requirements Timeline

Critical Compliance Requirements

PDPL 2025: Data localization required. Customer data must be stored in Vietnam. Lease Tier 3 data center (Viettel IDC).
Cross-Border Transfer: File CTIA with Ministry of Public Security before transferring sales data to Dhaka/Singapore.
DTI Law Benefits: Effective Jan 1, 2026. Tax breaks, streamlined procedures for digital tech enterprises.

Compliance Risk Mitigation

  • Partner with top-tier law firm (Baker McKenzie Vietnam) for IRC/ERC
  • Engage compliance consultant for PDPL implementation
  • Establish data center partnership (Viettel IDC)
  • Develop internal compliance team with local expertise
  • Regular audits and compliance reviews
