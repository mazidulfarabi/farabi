Critical Compliance Requirements

PDPL 2025 Compliance: The Personal Data Protection Law requires data localization. Customer data (names, phone numbers for warranty) must be stored in Vietnam. Smart Tech must lease server space in a local Tier 3 data center (e.g., Viettel IDC).
Cross-Border Data Transfer: Before transferring any sales data back to HQ in Dhaka or hubs in Singapore, a Cross-Border Transfer Impact Assessment (CTIA) must be filed with the Ministry of Public Security.
DTI Law Benefits: The Law on Digital Technology Industry (effective January 1, 2026) provides tax breaks and streamlined procedures for digital technology enterprises, reducing compliance costs.

Compliance Risk Mitigation

  • Partner with top-tier local law firm (e.g., Baker McKenzie Vietnam) to navigate IRC/ERC process
  • Engage local compliance consultant for PDPL implementation
  • Establish data center partnership with Viettel IDC or equivalent
  • Develop internal compliance team with local expertise
  • Regular audits and compliance reviews
