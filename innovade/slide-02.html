Malaysia: The Efficiency Seeker's Trap

Strengths: Mature economy, 2nd in ASEAN digital readiness. ICT market $28.65B.

Critical Barrier: 30% Bumiputera equity requirement conflicts with operational control.

Market Saturation: Highly consolidated. Fierce price wars.

Philippines: The Consumption Engine

Strengths: GDP 5-6%. BPO drives ICT demand. Market $30.16B (11% CAGR).

Challenges: Complex FINL, high operational costs. Lower Ease of Doing Business.

Institutional Risk: Bureaucracy, port congestion, corruption.

Vietnam: The Manufacturing Superpower

Strengths: GDP 7-8% (fastest-growing). ICT market $165.9B. 100% foreign ownership.

Regulatory: DTI Law 2026 provides tax breaks. PDPL requires data localization.

Market Drivers: Samsung, Intel, Foxconn create B2B demand. Manufacturing FDI drives IT needs.

Bangladesh Lens: Bilateral trade $1B+. FTA negotiations. Similar trajectory.
