Malaysia: The Efficiency Seeker's Trap

Strengths: Mature economy, 2nd in ASEAN digital readiness. ICT market $28.65B driven by cloud, cybersecurity, big data.

Critical Barrier: 30% Bumiputera equity requirement for WRT license conflicts with operational control needs.

Market Saturation: Highly consolidated (Formis, Mesiniaga). Fierce price wars for new entrants.

Philippines: The Consumption Engine

Strengths: GDP 5-6%. BPO sector drives ICT hardware demand. Market $30.16B with 11% CAGR.

Challenges: Complex FINL, high operational costs (logistics, energy). Lower Ease of Doing Business ranking.

Institutional Risk: Bureaucratic inefficiency, port congestion, corruption challenges.
