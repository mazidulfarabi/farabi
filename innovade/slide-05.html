Strategic Framework Justification for Vietnam Selection

Porter's Diamond Model

Factor Conditions: Large cost-effective labor force. Data center costs: $6.9M per MW (lowest in region). Facilitates cloud/server market growth.
Demand Conditions: Global demand. Samsung, Intel, Foxconn create insatiable B2B demand for IT infrastructure. Unique "derivative demand".
Related Industries: Dense ecosystem: logistics, industrial parks, suppliers. 5G/cloud infrastructure (Viettel, VNPT) supports networking gear distribution.
Firm Strategy: Fierce competition (Digiworld, FPT) drives efficiency. Enter as "challenger" with better financing/SLAs.
